Governance
==========

Decision Making
---------------
We seek consensus among contributors and maintainers. For RFCs, maintainers facilitate reviews. Final decisions rest with maintainers, with dissent documented in the RFC PR.

Roles
-----
- Contributors: Propose RFCs, participate in discussions.
- Maintainers: Curate roadmap, facilitate RFCs, make final calls.
- Domain Owners: Component-specific approvers; listed in `CODEOWNERS`.

Meetings & Timelines
--------------------
RFC review windows typically last 1–2 weeks. Urgent proposals can be expedited. Synchronous meetings may be scheduled for contentious topics; conclusions are captured in the PR.

Amendments
----------
Changes to this governance are proposed via RFC or PR to this document.
